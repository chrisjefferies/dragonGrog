Select Equipment

To roll your starting gold (anywhere from 5 to 240, depending on class) click below. For simplicity, all prices and values are displayed in Copper Value. Weight for everything is calculated (all weight values for MED unless otherwise noted). Weapons and Armor will populate slots on the equipment character sheet.

Note: items with quantity always appear at the bottom of the list. This is just a strange artifact of how the functionality of the section was coded.

Instructions

Items, weapons and armor with a gold value WORK, Items with a — symbol DO NOT. Weapons and Armor with a — symbol followed by a gold value work, but will not populate the appropriate slots on the character sheet.
