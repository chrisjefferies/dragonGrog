Happy Brewing!

Thanks for using DragonGrog - the fastest fully fleshed out character generator this side of the Mountains of Doom.

Heads up — hitting refresh will dump all your progress. You've been warned.

We store none of your info - save it or print it if you want to keep it.

As of Version 1.0.0, almost all of the functionality for the core rules is working. A handful of items and equipment aren't functioning, and there is currently no display of item characteristics like damage or weight (consult your favorite rulebook, or check out Adventuring Gear, Weapons, or Armor over on the SRD wiki).

Rule updates and other additions for Pathfinder are coming! They are currently about 30% complete. Contact us if you're interested in donating either dev skillz or money to help speed things along.

Once you're done, Custom Character sheets will automatically generate. Hit print in your browser's interface and set sail with no headers/footers, no margins and background images and colors set to display. Everything else will automatically disappear.
