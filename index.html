All the information taken in by the engine populates onto a character sheet that is customized to your character, and print ready!

We've got loads of neat things
in the works

Soon, you'll be able to customize and print blank character sheets, change character sheet fonts and styling, and write notes and other information directly onto a populated character sheet.

Dungeon Masters will also love the ability to build mini-NPC sheets even faster, generate random magic items with the touch of a button, and generate custom magic items that their PC's can add to their characters by simply inputing a unique 10-character ID Code.

We're also in the R&D phase of developing a login system where you can store your character's information and come back to it later, though it's looking like a long-term project that may require some external funding.
